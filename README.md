# MapsToDo-Public

Maps To Do lets anyone plan and organize map jobs. Quickly measure the area, circumference, and distance on the map. Easily save to-do items, take notes, and photos of measurements. And with advanced sharing features, itâ€™s easy to share photos and send files of measurements to family, friends, and co-workers.
